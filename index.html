History

The Cripperelli Family was formed in 2000 by Carmela Velenti. Carmela really loved the Italian Mafia and wanted to join, but there were a few reasons she couldn’t. A. she isn’t Italian, B. she is a she, C. she has Spinal Muscular Atrophy. Carmela decided to change her so she would seem Italian. At summer camp she talked it over with her friend Gia and they decided to put a plan into motion… create their own mafia and their own family. They call themselves the Cripperelli Family aka Crip Fam.
They set up their standards, you must have a form of muscular dystrophy and in order to become an associate you must complete three tasks. The founding family included:

  • Boss - Carmela Valenti
  • Underboss/Consigliere - Giovanna Lococo
  • Sonny Scarfo
  • Fat Joe
  • Aide- Frank
